Why is it Good?

Freshness and Simple Production

Kesong puti is a good choice because it's a fresh cheese without preservatives, offering a pure, natural flavor.

Rich in Nutrients

It is a great source of essential nutrients, particularly protein and calcium, from the rich carabao's milk.

Cultural and Culinary Importance

Kesong puti is a cultural staple in the Philippines, often enjoyed as a classic breakfast food.

Versatility in the Kitchen

Its mild, slightly salty flavor makes it a versatile ingredient for both sweet and savory dishes.
